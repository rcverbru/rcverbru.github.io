## Primary Research Areas

- Localization in adverse weather
- Species detection and classification for bat habitat conservation
- Lidar benchmarkingand data collection in adverse winter weather
- ARPA-E NextCar II, road surface analysis and data collection
- Winter Snow dataset for LiDAR systems and neural network training for vehicle detection in heavy snow environments
- Bipedal locomotion and gate correction on low mu surfaces
- Calculating fractional order calculus through usage of symmetric neural networks
